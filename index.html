This site is made by and for those who loved and admired Ellen Moss Robinson. Ellen passed away in the fall of 2025, leaving a global community of family and friends grieving but filled with warm memories.

She was humbly brillant and uniquely capable of mastering a tremendously wide range of diverse subjects, from arts to engineering, from science to literature, from carpentry to landscaping.

She was a devoted, loving, and patient partner to her husband and two sons, and a committed and thoughtful daughter, sister, and friend. We count ourselves fortunate to have been included in her circle.
